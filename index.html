A website about ducks.

Duck is the common name for numerous species of waterfowl in the family Anatidae. Ducks are generally smaller and shorter-necked than swans and geese, which are members of the same family.

Ducks are mostly aquatic birds living in both fresh water and sea water and found on every continent except for Antarctica..

Ducks are omnivores.

A common urban legend is that a ducks quack does not echo. This has however, been proven to be false.
